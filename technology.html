Spiritual Records Mobile App

Wondering what to do with my time and my tech skills (or lack of), I was lead to find value in working for my friends. This app was a passion project, done by me from scratch. And it actually made it to release!

The purpose of the app is to provide an online portal to the work and art that comes out of Spiritual Bar and Records.

Find the app on the iOS and Google Play app stores.

Artwork used to the right is by Jo Wright and available as an A3 print.

Technical Experience

NaturalNet
January 2018 - Present

NaturalNet is my dissertation projet at Imperial College. NaturalNet is an Android application which utilises decentralised networking (and opportunistic networking) techniques to solve communication issues during a natural disaster.

Through exploring case studies of natural disasters I identified flaws in disaster preparation and response, and am addressing these issues with the application.

The networking element is addressed through a modified version of OppNet, an opportunistic networking service.


Experience Gained

  • Android application development
  • Decentralised and opportunistic networking development and design
  • Modelling networks and simulating networking protocols
